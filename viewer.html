How to Annotate

Click-to-Annotate (Action Annotations)

  1. Click on a player in any video tile. All videos pause and a red marker appears where you clicked.
  2. Use ← → arrow keys (or the step buttons) to move frame-by-frame.
  3. Press S or click Mark Start to set the start frame.
  4. Step to the end frame, then press E or click Mark End.
  5. Press Enter or click Confirm to open the annotation form.
  6. You can reposition the marker by clicking a different spot on the video before confirming.
  7. Press Esc or click Cancel to abort and resume playback.

Drill Annotations (S/E Shortcut)

  1. While the video is playing, press S to mark the start time.
  2. Press E to mark the end time.
  3. The annotation form appears automatically.
  4. Select a Drill type instead of an Action (or both).

Annotation Form

  • Player — Select the player from the roster dropdown.
  • Action — Choose the stroke type (forehand, backhand, serve, etc.) or "None" for drill-only annotations.
  • Drill — Optionally select a drill type: Ground Strokes, Net Play, or Serves.
  • Perfect stroke — Check this box if the stroke was executed perfectly.
  • Notes — Add any free-form observations.

Playback Controls

  • Space — Play / Pause all videos
  • Speed buttons — 0.5x, 1x, 2x playback speed
  • Seek slider — Drag to jump to any point in the session
  • Volume — Adjust per-video using the slider below each tile

Saving

Click Save to Dropbox to upload your annotations. They are saved as both JSON and CSV, and will auto-load next time you open this session.
